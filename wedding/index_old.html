Jesi and Clint's Wedding

9/3/2016

On Saturday, September 3rd, 2016, Jesi and Clint are getting married at Camp Nuhop near Mansfield, Ohio.

The Ceremony, reception, and wedding party will all take place at the camp and there is free lodging available in the cabins for any guests who want to stay at camp that night.

There is also a State Park Resort Hotel a short walk or drive from the camp if you would prefer to stay there.
